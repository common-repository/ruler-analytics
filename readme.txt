== Description ==

# Measure your Marketing

Do you know where your leads, sales & phone calls come from?

_Try Ruler Analytics for free to discover this and more..._

Ruler Analytics makes measuring your marketing activities simple.
Ruler allows you to track every conversion (lead or sale), company who visited your site _and_ phone call back to
the original source from which it was generated including the website, search engine and keyword.

For more information visit:

[Ruler Analytics](http://www.ruleranalytics.com)

== Installation ==

1. Upload `ruleranalytics` directory to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Add the site_id from Ruler Analytics to the settings (Admin > Settings > Ruler Analytics)

== Screenshots ==
1. After installation choose the Ruler Analytics option from the Admin Settings menu.
2. Enter your Ruler Analytics site ID into the screen as specified.
3. Measure your marketing
